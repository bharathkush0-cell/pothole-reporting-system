About RoadWatch

A modern civic-tech platform built for municipalities, ward officers, and citizens — bringing pothole reporting, AI severity scoring, crew dispatch, and predictive maintenance into one unified workflow.

For Citizens

Report damaged roads in seconds with photo, GPS, and AI-assisted severity.

For Crews

Prioritized work queue with size, depth, and material estimates.

For Administrators

Analytics, hotspot prediction, exports, and audit-ready records.

Built for civic scale
